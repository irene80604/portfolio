Aegon · 2024

Empowering Stress-Free Retirement Decisions

How we redesigned Aegon's pension payout calculator to help elderly users make confident financial choices without panic.

The Challenge

Retirement should be joyful, but it often becomes a coordination challenge as people need to figure out what to do with pension savings.

Conducted 11 in-depth interviews (ages 60–65), with 6 dedicated to accessibility users. Our goal was to uncover hidden friction in the cognitive processing of financial data.
